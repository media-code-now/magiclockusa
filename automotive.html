Emergency Lockout Service

Unexpectedly locked out? You can count on us to be there for you with emergency locksmith services 24 hours, 7 days a week! Magic Lock USA is a top emergency locksmith in Charlotte with a consistent average response time of less than 30 minutes. We can replace your lost or stolen keys without a problem as well as rekey it to make sure no one drives off with your car when you're not in it! With us you don't need to worry about hidden fees or extra charges during holidays or weekends - just give us a call anytime and we'll be there!

Transponder Programming

You don't need to go to the dealer to reprogram your transponder key anymore! Magic Lock USA can take care of it for you for just a fraction of the dealer's cost. Whether you have a domestic or import make car or truck, we have the expertise to program your transponder key.

Keyless Entry

Magic Lock USA is your one-stop-shop for factory keyless entry remotes, programming services and key fobs. Offering the widest variety of OEM remote controls in the industry and serving virtually all imports and domestic makes of cars or truck - we are the right choice when it comes to programming a keyless entry to your motor vehicle.

Motorcycle Keys

At Magic Lock USA, we keep stock of almost all motorcycle manufacturer key blanks in order to easily replace or duplicate your existing motorcycle keys. Whether you come to our shop or we meet you at your location, we can get the job done quickly and professionally.

Magic Lock USA provides the highest quality of automotive locksmith services with high-end tools, skilled professionals and friendly customer service. We understand the urgency of your needs and exceed expectations every step of the way.
